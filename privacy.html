Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KM Makes LLC (“KM Makes”, "we," "us," "our") handles information in connection with your use of CRWD SRFR (the "Site"). We've kept this short because CRWD SRFR is built to collect as little personal information as possible.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require accounts, logins, or personal information to use CRWD SRFR. You can browse events, search, and filter without providing any personal data.

The limited data we do encounter comes from:

a) Cloudflare Web Analytics: We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand overall Site traffic (e.g., page views, general visit counts, referring sites, approximate geographic region, device/browser type). This tool is privacy-friendly by design:

  • It does not use cookies,
  • It does not track you across other websites,
  • It does not collect personal data or build a profile of individual visitors.

It gives us aggregate, anonymized insight into how the Site is used — not who you are.

b) Hosting Logs (GitHub Pages / Cloudflare): Like virtually any website, our hosting and CDN infrastructure (GitHub Pages and/or Cloudflare) may automatically log basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and request timestamps, for security and operational purposes. We do not directly access or use these logs to identify individuals.

c) Donations via PayPal: If you choose to make a donation through the Site, you'll be directed to PayPal to complete the transaction. We do not collect or store your payment information, card details, or PayPal account information ourselves — that is handled entirely by PayPal under their own privacy policy. We may see limited, non-sensitive transaction confirmation (e.g., that a donation was made), but not your full financial details.

2. How We Use Information

The limited data described above is used only to:

  • Understand aggregate Site usage and improve the experience,
  • Maintain the security and performance of the Site,
  • Process voluntary donations (via PayPal, as described above).

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We don't have any data to sell even if we wanted to — we simply don't collect enough to build profiles on visitors.

3. Cookies

CRWD SRFR does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Cloudflare Web Analytics is cookieless. If this changes in the future (e.g., if we add features that require cookies), this Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Third-Party Links

The Site links out to third-party venue and ticketing websites (e.g., AXS, SeatGeek, opendate.io, and others) for event details and ticket purchases. Once you leave CRWD SRFR, your activity is governed by that third party's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review those policies before providing any personal information on those sites.

5. Children's Privacy

CRWD SRFR is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Site, please contact us so we can address it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data associated with the Site, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Because we collect very little personal data, your overall exposure through CRWD SRFR is low — but we can't guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to:
