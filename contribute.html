Contribute to Crwd Srfr

Why Contribute?

CRWD SRFR is a project started and run by one guy (me, I'm the guy) with the hope of serving the Cleveland music scene and beyond.

As a solo designer and developer, I'm happy to support this project by my own means, but the truth is that I can always use as much as help as I can get. Hosting and maintaining the site takes time and resources, and the more support I have beyond my own better guarantees it's continued existence and quality.

Which is why I ask you to consider contributing to the site, if you have the means to do so! Any little bit helps, and your support encourages me to continue building more and better products.

Either way, whether you contribute or just use the site, thank you for being a part of CRWD SRFR!
