Contribute to Crwd Srfr

Why Contribute?

CRWD SRFR is a project started and run by one guy (me, I'm the guy) with the hope of serving the Cleveland entertainment scene and beyond.

As a solo designer and developer, I'm happy to support this project by my own means, but the truth is that I'll always take as much as help as I can get. Hosting and maintaining the site takes time and resources, and the more support I have beyond my own better guarantees it's continued existence and quality.

Which is why I ask you to consider contributing to the site, if you have the means to do so! Any little bit helps, and your support encourages me to continue building more and better products.

Either way, whether you contribute or just use the site, thank you for being a part of CRWD SRFR!

How to Contribute

Feature a Show on CRWD SRFR:

If you are interested in promoting a show or event on our "Featured" page, please contact us. Founding rates start at $25 per week.

Sponsor CRWD SRFR:

If you are interested in becoming a sponsor of CRWD SRFR, please contact us. Sponsorship includes your logo at the end of our "Calendar" page, as well as a discount on "Featured" spaces. Founding rates start at $60 per month.
