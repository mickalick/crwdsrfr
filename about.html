About CRWD SRFR

One Live Show Calendar to Rule Them All.

Just kidding. But not really.

CRWD SRFR is a website for finding live shows happening in the greater Cleveland, OH area. Established in Summer 2026, CRWD SRFR was inspired by the many live music and event calendars scattered across the internet that try (and fail) to list every show happening in your area in an easy to read and searchable format.

With both our venue list and calendar updated twice a week, CRWD SRFR aims to be your one stop shop for finding every show happening near you!
